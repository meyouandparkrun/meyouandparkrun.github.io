Carisbrooke, South Australia

Salisbury is a northern suburb in Adelaide, South Australia. It is the seat of the City of Salisbury, and in the South Australian Legislative Assembly electoral district of Ramsay and the Australian House of Representatives division of Spence

We felt like trying one of Trents more 'local' Parkruns and decided to give Carisbrooke a try. We didn't have high expectations as it's located on the outskirts of some more questionable suburbs, so were blown away with this underated course! The trail is mostly gravel and folows a stream for the good majority. The course is on the lower side of the stream so we were shaded on both passes. The park itself is quite large and well mantained, with large sections of green grass and well established tress providing good coverage. Someone bought their golden retriever along and had it wear a volunteer vest. It was super friendly and very pattable.

Trent: 6th place @ 23:59 (5th male & 1st in age category)
Sara: 10th place @ 25:22 (2nd female & rd in age category)
