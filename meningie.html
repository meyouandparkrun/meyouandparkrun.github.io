Meningie, South Australia

Meningie is a town on the south-east side of Lake Albert in South Australia. It is on the Princes Highway near The Coorong and was surveyed in 1866. At the 2016 census, the locality of Meningie had a population of 1118 with a median age of 51 while its town centre had a population of 852.

SATURDAYS are for Parkrun! We couldn't have picked a better parkrun to start our roadtrip. With a total of 7 runners, including ourselves, we found this community so welcoming and excited to have visitors. It was quite the chilly morning, but we were lucky enough to have full sun beaming down. This trail followed a cemented bike/walking path along Lake Albert with the start and finish on the grass of Lions Jubilee Park. The group invited us out for coffee after the run, where we got heaps and heaps of recommendations of where to visit as we left their cute little town.
