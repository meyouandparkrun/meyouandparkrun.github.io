Tuggeranong, Australian Capital Territory

The District of Tuggeranong is one of the original eighteen districts of the Australian Capital Territory used in land administration. The district is subdivided into divisions, sections and blocks and is the southernmost town centre of Canberra, the capital city of Australia.
